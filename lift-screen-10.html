About App
  • We believe that LIFT is the first of its kind in India. LIFT provides a monthly digest of select statutes, both central and state(presently Tamilnadu alone), case laws of the Supreme Court of India and High Courts (Presently Madras High Court alone), in English and other respective regional languages (Presently Tamil Alone). LIFT also presents Legal Forum which are motivating and useful for all concerned. LIFT presents the case laws in unique and narrative manner so as to bring out the exact issue on hand and make it easy for the readers to understand and remember. Similarly all essential tenets of new statues are provided in a systemic manner. The contents of LIFT is from the Team of practicing lawyers with standing of around 20 years and more and hence we believe that we can understand the expectations of the user and deliver a quality product.
