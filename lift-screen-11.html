Law In Finger Tips
How To Use
  • How to use - LIFT is a user friendly app, with mainly three segments, namely Statutes, Case Laws and Legal Forum .

    Upon selecting the LIFT of the month icon, the user can access the Statutes, Case Laws and Legal Forum reported for the current month.

    The contents pertaining to the previous months will be available in the LIFT LIBRARY.

    Search can be made in the current as well as LIBRARY by choosing the category and entering the related word.

    LIFT content is available in English and Tamil(presently) and the same can be chosen by selecting the Settings icon and selecting the language.

    The app is web-based and once the mobile is connected to the internet and LIFT is used, the latest contents will automatically be uploaded and can be viewed.
